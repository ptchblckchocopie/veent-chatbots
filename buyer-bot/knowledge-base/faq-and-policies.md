## Frequently Asked Questions

### How will I receive my tickets after purchasing?
Your tickets are delivered automatically to your registered email address and are also accessible through your Veent Tix account under "My Tickets." You may present your digital ticket (QR code) at the event entrance, or print a physical copy. Ensure your email and account details are accurate before completing your order.

### Can I request a refund or exchange after purchasing a ticket?
All sales made through Veent Tix are final and non-refundable. Once a ticket has been purchased, no refunds, cancellations, or exchanges are granted for any reason — including changes in personal schedule, transportation issues, illness, dissatisfaction with the event, or weather conditions. If an event is officially canceled by the organizer, refund requests are handled directly by the event organizer according to their own refund policy.

### What happens if the event is postponed, rescheduled, or canceled?
If an event is postponed or rescheduled, your existing ticket remains valid for the new date or venue. Veent Tix will send an email notification once the organizer finalizes updated details. If the event is fully canceled, the event organizer is responsible for handling refund requests or alternative solutions such as event credits.

### What payment methods are accepted?
Veent Tix accepts: Visa, Mastercard, GCash, Maya, GrabPay, BillEase, and Bank Transfer.

### How do I create an account?
Visit https://www.veent.io and click "Login" to access the sign-in page. You can sign in with Google or create a new account with your email.

### How can I contact Veent Tix for support?
Send an email to support@veenttix.com with your full name, registered email address, and ticket order ID (if applicable). Customer support is available Monday to Saturday, 9:00 AM to 6:00 PM (PHT), with responses within 24 to 48 hours.

### How do I find the event organizer's contact information?
Each event page has a "NEED HELP? CONTACT US" section at the bottom. To find it: go to the event page (e.g., eventname.veent.net), scroll down to the bottom of the page, and look for the organizer's name and contact details (phone number or Facebook page). The page footer also shows "Hosted by [organizer name]".

### Is my personal information safe?
Yes. Veent Tix uses SSL encryption, firewalls, secure cloud hosting, and role-based access controls to protect your data. Personal information is collected and processed in compliance with the Philippine Data Privacy Act of 2012. For details, see the privacy policy at https://www.veent.io/data-privacy.

### Can I buy multiple tickets in one order?
Yes, most events allow bulk registration. You can purchase multiple tickets per order, typically up to 10 tickets depending on the organizer's settings.

### What if I lose my ticket?
Your digital ticket is always accessible through your Veent Tix account. You can also search your email for the confirmation message containing your QR code. If you still have trouble, contact support@veenttix.com.
