## What is Veent Tix?

Veent Tix is an online ticketing platform operated by Veent Apps Inc. that allows event organizers to create, promote, and sell tickets for concerts, festivals, fun runs, workshops, and other live events. Customers can browse upcoming events, purchase tickets through a secure payment system, and receive digital tickets via email or their Veent Tix account.

Website: https://www.veent.io
Discover Events: https://www.veent.io/discover

## Key Features

- **Easy Event Setup**: Organizers can create and manage events with tools designed for success.
- **Advanced Ticketing**: Flexible ticket types, early bird pricing, and promotional codes.
- **Secure Payments**: Enterprise-grade security with industry-leading encryption. Accepted methods include Visa, Mastercard, GCash, Maya, GrabPay, BillEase, and Bank Transfer.
- **Real-Time Analytics**: Track sales, attendance, and engagement metrics in real-time.
- **Marketing Tools**: Integrated email campaigns, social sharing, and promotional features.
- **QR Code Tickets**: Digital tickets with QR codes for fast event check-in.

## How to Buy Tickets

1. Visit https://www.veent.io/discover to browse upcoming events
2. Click on an event to view details, date, venue, and ticket prices
3. Select your preferred ticket type and quantity
4. Fill in the registration form with your details
5. Choose a payment method and complete payment
6. Receive your digital ticket (QR code) via email and in your Veent Tix account
7. Present your QR code (digital or printed) at the event entrance

## How to Create an Event

If you're an organizer who wants to host an event on Veent Tix:

1. Go to https://dashboard.veent.io and sign up or log in
2. Click "Create Event" on the dashboard home page
3. Fill in event details: title, date & time, location, description, and poster image
4. Set up ticket types with names, prices, and stock quantities
5. Customize your registration form if needed
6. Set the event status to "Live" to start accepting registrations
7. Your event will be published at a unique URL (e.g., your-event.veent.net)

For organizer inquiries, visit https://www.veent.io/contacts or email support@veenttix.com.
