Event Organizer Contact Information for Veent Tix Events

How to find organizer contact info on an event page:
1. Go to the event page (e.g., eventname.veent.net)
2. Scroll down to the bottom of the page
3. Look for the "NEED HELP? CONTACT US" section
4. The organizer name and contact info (phone or Facebook) will be shown there
5. The page footer also shows "Hosted by [organizer name]"
